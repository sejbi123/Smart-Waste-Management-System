## Stakeholders Identification – Smart Waste Management System (ECO Tirana)

| Stakeholder | Role / Responsibility | Importance | Influence | Interests / Positive Impacts | Concerns |
|--------------|----------------------|------------|-----------|------------------------------|----------|
| Waste Management Manager | Uses dashboard to monitor operations, approve schedules and review analytics. | High | High | Real-time visibility of bins, truck locations and schedules. Reduces manual processes. | Data accuracy of sensors and resistance to workflow changes. |
| Truck Driver | Receives optimized routes, confirms collections and reports issues. | High | Medium | Clear navigation, less paperwork and fewer wasted trips. | Fear of tracking and possible connectivity issues. |
| Field Operator (Bin Technician) | Maintains bins and logs maintenance updates in the system. | Medium | Low-Medium | Digital work orders and faster reporting with photos. | Field conditions and extra digital steps. |
| Citizen of Tirana | Reports full bins or illegal dumping via public form. | Medium | Low | Cleaner city and transparency in reports. | Privacy concerns and doubt about response. |
| ECO Tirana Management | Reviews KPIs, oversees costs and strategic decisions. | High | High | Cost reduction and improved service quality. | ROI justification and staff adoption. |
| Municipality of Tirana | Receives compliance and performance reports. | Medium | Medium | Verified regulatory reports and environmental compliance. | Data reliability and integration requirements. |
| Waste Disposal Facility Manager | Manages landfill or recycling facilities and tracks waste deposits. | Medium-High | Medium | Better facility planning and waste tracking. | Integration complexity and data accuracy. |
